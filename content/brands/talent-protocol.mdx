Talent Protocol is a decentralized infrastructure platform that revolutionizes how professional reputation and career development are managed and monetized, providing blockchain-based reputation scoring, talent tokenization that allows investment in individual careers, credential verification systems, and career analytics that create portable professional reputations, enable merit-based talent discovery, and connect talented individuals with mentors, investors, and opportunities globally.
