Parfois is a Portuguese fashion accessories brand with a global retail footprint spanning 1,100+ stores across 70 countries. Founded in 1994 in Porto, the brand offers trendy accessories, bags, jewelry, watches, and footwear at accessible prices. Parfois has built a strong digital presence with 1.4 million monthly organic visitors and over 2 million Instagram followers, becoming one of Portugal's most successful fashion retail exports.
