# Lime

Lime is a pioneering shared electric mobility platform that has transformed urban transportation through its network of electric scooters and bikes. The company has built comprehensive infrastructure for micro-mobility solutions that provide sustainable, convenient, and accessible transportation options in cities worldwide.

## Mobility Infrastructure

Lime operates a comprehensive shared mobility ecosystem including:

- **Electric Scooters**: Fleet of GPS-enabled electric scooters deployed across urban areas
- **Electric Bikes**: Pedal-assist and electric bicycles integrated with the same platform infrastructure
- **Mobile Platform**: User-friendly app for locating, unlocking, and paying for rides
- **Charging Network**: Distributed charging and maintenance infrastructure to keep vehicles operational
- **City Partnerships**: Collaboration frameworks with municipal governments for regulated deployment

## Urban Transportation Impact

The platform addresses critical urban mobility challenges:

- **Last-Mile Solution**: Bridging the gap between public transportation and final destinations
- **Environmental Impact**: Reducing carbon emissions by providing alternatives to car trips
- **Traffic Congestion**: Decreasing urban traffic through efficient short-distance transportation
- **Accessibility**: Making transportation more accessible and affordable for diverse urban populations
- **Infrastructure Integration**: Working with cities to integrate micro-mobility into broader transportation planning

Lime represents the evolution of urban transportation infrastructure from car-centric models to diverse, sustainable, and technology-enabled mobility ecosystems that better serve modern city populations.
