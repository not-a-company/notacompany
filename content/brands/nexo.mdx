# Nexo

Nexo is a leading cryptocurrency wealth management platform that has been pioneering digital asset financial services since 2018. The platform provides comprehensive infrastructure for earning, borrowing, spending, and managing cryptocurrency portfolios, serving as a bridge between traditional financial services and the digital asset ecosystem.

## Platform Infrastructure

Nexo offers a complete suite of financial infrastructure services including:

- **Flexible Savings**: Daily compound interest earning on cryptocurrency holdings with up to 16% annual yields
- **Credit Lines**: Crypto-backed lending starting from 2.9% APR, enabling liquidity without selling digital assets
- **Exchange Services**: Trading platform supporting 100+ digital assets with integrated cashback rewards
- **Payment Solutions**: Nexo Card offering both debit and credit functionality with up to 2% cashback
- **Institutional Services**: Corporate accounts, OTC trading, and white-label solutions for businesses

## Market Leadership

Nexo has established itself as a major player in cryptocurrency financial infrastructure:

- **Assets Under Management**: Over $11 billion in digital assets under management
- **Global Presence**: Operating in 150+ jurisdictions with regulatory compliance
- **Client Base**: Serving individuals, businesses, and institutions with 24/7 customer support
- **Transaction Volume**: Processed over $371 billion in transactions since inception
- **Loyalty Program**: Tiered benefits system providing enhanced rates and services for higher-balance clients

## Financial Innovation

The platform addresses key infrastructure needs in digital asset management:

- **Yield Generation**: Enabling passive income through cryptocurrency staking and lending mechanisms
- **Liquidity Solutions**: Providing access to fiat currency without requiring asset sales
- **Portfolio Management**: Comprehensive tools for diversifying and managing digital asset portfolios
- **Regulatory Compliance**: Operating within legal frameworks to provide institutional-grade security
- **Cross-Border Payments**: Facilitating global transactions and remittances through cryptocurrency infrastructure

Nexo represents the maturation of cryptocurrency financial services infrastructure, providing the comprehensive tools and services necessary for digital assets to function as a legitimate wealth management and financial services ecosystem.
