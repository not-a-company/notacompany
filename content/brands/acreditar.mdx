# Acreditar

Acreditar is Portugal's leading non-profit organization dedicated to supporting children and adolescents with cancer and their families. The foundation operates as a comprehensive support infrastructure, providing both immediate assistance and long-term care solutions for families facing pediatric cancer diagnoses.

## Mission and Services

Acreditar provides multi-dimensional support through:

- **Medical Support**: Complementary healthcare services and medical assistance coordination
- **Psychosocial Care**: Professional psychological and social support for patients and families
- **Accommodation Solutions**: Housing facilities near treatment centers to keep families together during treatment
- **Educational Continuity**: Programs ensuring children can continue their education during treatment
- **Family Support**: Financial assistance and practical support for families during treatment periods

## Infrastructure Impact

The organization operates as critical healthcare infrastructure in Portugal's pediatric oncology ecosystem:

- **National Network**: Comprehensive coverage across Portugal's major medical centers
- **Partnership Ecosystem**: Deep integration with hospitals, medical professionals, and other healthcare organizations
- **Resource Optimization**: Efficient allocation of resources to maximize impact for families in need
- **Community Integration**: Building support networks that extend beyond immediate medical needs

Acreditar represents the evolution of non-profit healthcare support from basic assistance to comprehensive infrastructure that addresses the full spectrum of challenges faced by families dealing with pediatric cancer.
