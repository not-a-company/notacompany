Feira dos Tecidos is Portugal's premier textile and fabric retail chain, founded over 40 years ago and now operating 18 stores nationwide. The company serves everyone from professional tailors and fashion designers to home crafters and interior decorators, offering an extensive catalog of fabrics, sewing supplies, and haberdashery. Known for competitive pricing and expert staff who understand textiles, Feira dos Tecidos has built a loyal customer base across generations with a 4.4/5 Trustpilot rating.
