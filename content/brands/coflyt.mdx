CoFlyt is intelligent aircraft software developed by pilots that provides peace of mind for aircraft owners, partnerships, and flying clubs by streamlining aircraft ownership with centralized flight logs, flight map tracking, usage analysis, aircraft availability calendaring, maintenance alerts to A&Ps, payment simplification, and real-time compliance monitoring, enabling owners to know exactly when maintenance is needed and access comprehensive usage data from their mobile device.
