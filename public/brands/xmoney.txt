## About xMoney


xMoney is a financial infrastructure platform specializing in stablecoin payment processing and fiat currency conversion, serving as a critical bridge between traditional banking systems and the stablecoin economy through advanced payment processing, streamlined on-ramp and off-ramp services, regulatory-compliant infrastructure, and developer-friendly APIs that enable third-party platforms to integrate stablecoin functionality, providing the rails necessary for mainstream adoption of digital asset payments in real-world commerce and financial applications.
