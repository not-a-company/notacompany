## About CoFlyt


CoFlyt is a shared finance platform for general aviation partnerships that simplifies the financial complexity of aircraft co-ownership. The platform handles cost allocation, expense splitting, usage-based billing, and payment collection among partnership members, making it effortless for flying clubs and aircraft partnerships to manage shared ownership finances transparently and fairly.
