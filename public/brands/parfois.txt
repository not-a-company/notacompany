## About Parfois


Parfois is a Portuguese fashion accessories brand with a global retail footprint spanning 1,100+ stores across 70 countries. The brand has built a strong digital presence with 1.4 million monthly organic visitors and over 2 million Instagram followers. Through local search optimization services, Parfois enhanced their reputation infrastructure and multi-location search presence across Google Business Profile and local directories, ensuring each store is discoverable when customers search for fashion accessories nearby.
