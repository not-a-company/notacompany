## About Acreditar


Acreditar is Portugal's largest non-profit organization dedicated to supporting children and adolescents with cancer and their families, operating as comprehensive support infrastructure that provides medical assistance coordination, psychosocial care, accommodation solutions near treatment centers, educational continuity programs, and financial support, with nationwide coverage across Portugal's major medical centers and deep integration with hospitals and healthcare organizations to address the full spectrum of challenges faced by families dealing with pediatric cancer.
