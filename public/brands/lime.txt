## About Lime


Lime is a pioneering shared electric mobility platform that has transformed urban transportation through its network of GPS-enabled electric scooters and bikes, operating comprehensive micro-mobility infrastructure with a mobile platform for ride access, distributed charging and maintenance networks, and city partnerships that address last-mile transportation needs, reduce carbon emissions, decrease traffic congestion, and provide sustainable, accessible transportation alternatives in cities worldwide.
