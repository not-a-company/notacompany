## About Polkastarter


Polkastarter is a decentralized fundraising infrastructure platform that revolutionized how blockchain projects raise capital and launch tokens through a multi-chain launchpad ecosystem enabling Initial DEX Offerings (IDOs), democratized access to early-stage investments, and smart contract-based token distribution, establishing best practices for decentralized fundraising and becoming foundational DeFi infrastructure that connects investors, projects, and ecosystem participants globally.
