## About Songvice


Songvice was an early music industry platform that connected aspiring musicians and producers with experienced industry professionals for advisory and educational services, operating during the music industry's digital transformation and providing a producer marketplace, structured learning resources, remote collaboration tools, and networking features that democratized access to professional music production knowledge and explored concepts about technology-enabled music industry expertise that have since become standard in the modern music technology ecosystem.
